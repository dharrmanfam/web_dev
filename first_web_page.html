What is Hope Squad?

Our Mission

Hope Squad is a community of highschool committees that come together to give others hope and remind them they are loved.
Numbers of people are voted by people of the school to pick those who will help them with their lives both in and out of school.
Hope Squad prioritizes people's mental and physical health, hoping to establish long term healthy habits for them.
Our mission is to include everyone and anyone, reaching out to all no matter how unique or different they are.

What We Do

Hope Squad's team of students collaborates with school counselors to create enjoyable activities.
This helps students feel supported and builds connections between them and the team.
Hope Squad members also look for signs of struggle in their peers and alert trusted adults when needed.

Hope Squad Helps Deliver:

  • Motivation for students
  • Ensured safety
  • Engaging social activities
  • A positive, welcoming environment
  • Meaningful relationships with peers and teachers
