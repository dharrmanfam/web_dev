What is Hope Squad?

Our Mission

Hope Squad is a community of highschool committees that come together to give others hope and remind them they are loved.
Numbers of people are voted by people of the school to pick those who will help them with their lives both in and out of school.
Hope Squad prioritzes people's mental, physical health, in hope of establishing long term healthy habits for them.
Our mission is to include everyone and anyone, reaching out to everyone no matter how unique or different they are.

What we Do

Hope Squad's team of students come together with the counselors of the school to collaborate different fun
activites to do. This allows the students to see they are always available to talk together with. It also develops a bond
between the students and the team, both benefitting by mutual happinness from socializing. From further observaions
by Hope Squad members, their job is to keep everyone safe by recognizing signs and struggle in their peers, allowing
trusted adults to step in.

Hope Squad Helps Deliver:

  • Motivation for students
  • Ensure safety
  • Engaging activities for socialization
  • Positive environment for anyone and everyone
  • Intimate relationships with others including teachers
