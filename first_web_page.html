What is Hope Squad?

Our Mission

Hope Squad is a community of high school committees that come together to give others hope and remind them they are loved.
Students are voted by peers to choose those who will support and help others with challenges in and out of school.
Hope Squad prioritizes mental and physical health while promoting long-term healthy habits.
Our mission is to include everyone, reaching out regardless of differences.

What We Do

Hope Squad students collaborate with school counselors to create enjoyable activities.
These help students feel supported and build meaningful connections.
Hope Squad members also watch for signs of struggle and alert trusted adults.

Hope Squad Helps Deliver:

  • Motivation for students
  • Ensured safety
  • Engaging social activities
  • A positive, welcoming environment
  • Meaningful relationships with peers and teachers
Hope Squad Info

Hope Squad members work hard to create a safe and welcoming environment for every student. They organize events that help students feel included and valued, no matter their background. The program teaches students the importance of empathy, kindness, and support. Members also learn leadership skills and how to positively influence their peers. Through teamwork and dedication, Hope Squad strives to make a lasting impact on the school community.
